Registration fees are:

Early Registration by 11/30/2025 Late Registration by 02/26/2026
Undergradute students* BRL 80 BRL 100
Graduate students* BRL 100 BRL 150
  • * Students from the following Universities have 25% discount in the registration fee: Universidade Estadual do Ceará (UECE) use "STUDENTUECE" and Universidade de Brasília (UNB) use "STUDENTUNB".

  • *** For all students: you must upload in the registration system an official document from the University that you are currently a regular student or an official transcript file/school records.
